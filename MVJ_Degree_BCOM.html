B.Com - ACCOUNTING & FINANCE

B.Com - ACCOUNTING & FINANCE

The Bachelor of Commerce degree is designed to provide the student with a wide range of managerial skills while at the same time building competence in particular areas of business studies; where students are exposed to general business principles, taking courses in accounting/finance, auditing, income tax, statistics, marketing, economics, and information systems, understand how the money & financial world works, from personal finance, big businesses, to startups, national and international policies as well as economics in an efficient way.

Eligibility

Students who have completed their II PUC/ 12th Standard of CBSE/ISC or any other recognized Board above 60% Overall are eligible for admission to the programs.
