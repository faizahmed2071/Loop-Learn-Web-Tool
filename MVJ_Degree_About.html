About MVJ Degree College

MVJ Degree College

MVJ Degree College is an integral part of the MVJ Group of institutions, with a commitment to provide quality higher education. Our programs, infrastructure and teaching-learning are among the finest. The institution is affiliated to Bengaluru North University that offers various under graduate courses in the stream of Commerce, Management and Computer Applications and Science. Besides, various extra-curricular activities are planned to equip each individual with knowledge and self-confidence.

MVJ College was established in 1982 as the flagship institution to impart education in engineering, management, and beyond. A 15-acre campus in Whitefield, Bangalore, bears state-of-the-art infrastructure that creates an environment for progressive learning and development.

MVJ Degree College is affiliated to Bangalore North University, and offers programs as per the prescribed syllabus of the University. The college is centrally located in the Channasandra, ITPL Main Road, Bengaluru. The college offers under-graduate courses BCA(data science), B Sc Computer Science, B Com (Finance and Accounting), and BBA programmes.

The college plays a crucial role in entrenching a strong value system. The faculty today, makes optimum use of Information and Communication Technology (ICT). It has made a conscious effort to invest in facilities, hardware, and state of the art infrastructure and has made considerable advances in documentation and knowledge management. In keeping pace with global changes, the college has established a dynamic Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) to identify the strengths and weaknesses of the teaching-learning process which has helped the institution in its quest for excellence with emphasis on National and holistic development to confront the changing global standards and realize the objectives of the National Knowledge Commission.
