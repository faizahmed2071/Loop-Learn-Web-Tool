Principal's Message

Seasons Greetings

Our institution, initiated in 1970, stands as a testament to academic distinction, dedicated to serving a burgeoning student body pursuing degrees in various fields. With our commitment to fostering over 5000 young minds, we aim to set new benchmarks in the educational landscape.

At MVJ Group of Institutions, our genesis owes gratitude to the visionary legacy and educational commitment of our esteemed founder, the late Dr. M.V. Jayaraman. His enduring vision paved the way for multiple educational establishments in Bangalore, laying the foundation for our vibrant journey.

MVJ Degree College, nestled in the heart of Whitefield, prides itself on a faculty comprising seasoned professionals deeply dedicated to sculpting each student's educational journey. Our educators employ innovative teaching methodologies alongside cutting-edge technological resources, ensuring personalized attention to foster holistic growth for every learner. To sharpen and ensure holistic progress, we lay emphasis on cognitive, emotional, social and physical development, where students interact and learn from their peers.

A distinctive highlight of our academic program is the integration of internship opportunities starting from the first semester for all our students. This emphasis on practical experience alongside theoretical knowledge is a cornerstone of our curriculum. It aims to provide invaluable exposure and hands-on learning, preparing our students to excel in their chosen fields.

Our commitment extends beyond academic excellence; we strive to nurture individuals who embody intellectual acumen and ethical responsibility. Our holistic approach emphasizes comprehensive growth encompassing the mind, body, and spirit, preparing our students to thrive in a dynamic global landscape. With a dedicated team and a robust educational framework, MVJ Degree College is committed to guiding and shaping the leaders of tomorrow.

I extend my sincerest wishes for a future filled with brilliance, success, and accomplishments for each student.

Warm regards,

Dr. Tessy Tom M.Sc, M.Phil., PGDSSA , PhD
