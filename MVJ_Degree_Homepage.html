About Us

The legacy of MVJ Group of Institutions in serving the society through the medium of Education is well appreciated since 1972. With the emergence of 2024, MVJ Group of Institutions have achieved another milestone in the history with the approval of MVJ Degree College to explore new visions and new horizons for every young adult who enters its portals.

MVJ Degree College, affiliated to the Bengaluru North University, has multiplied possibilities and opportunities for its ever expanding student population. We firmly believe in empowering our students to become nation builders as well as leaders. In imparting quality education, we not only creating lifelong learners, but also instilling core values that respect diversity, tolerance, sincerity and integrity.
